  • Tonkotsu Ramen

    Ichiran Ramen Japan, 〒150-0041 Tokyo, Shibuya City, Jinnan, 1 Chome−22−7 岩本ビル B1F

    Famous for its pork bone creamy, soy sauce seasoned broth made it worth waiting. The broth is rich and noodles were made to my preferences. Accompanied by Asahi beer this is for sure a highlight in my Tokyo trip.

  • Curry Katsu set

    Tonkatsu Inaba Wako Narita Airport 1-1 Furugome, Narita, Chiba 282-0004, Japan

    This set has miso soup, shredded cabbage, a small bowl of radish and ginger, curry katsu itself and blend of spices to mash and include to your dish. The curry sauce was rich and worked well with the tonkatsu and rice. Finishing off the meal with warm miso soup got me ready for my long flight back to Calgary.

  • Mango Ice Halo-Halo

    HaloMango-Dmall XW7G+48H, D'mall de Boracay, Malay, Aklan, Philippines

    After a hot summer day in Boracay Philippines, this Mango flavoured Halo-Halo helped beat the heat and my craving for mangos. It contains shaved ice, fresh mango cubes, mango soft-serve, and fresh pinapple bits.
